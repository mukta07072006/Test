About Us

The World Amity Library, located in the heart of Moheshkhali, serves as a beacon of enlightenment for the local community. We provide free and inclusive access to books, research materials, and digital knowledge. Through cultural programs, youth initiatives, and collaborative projects, we empower individuals to learn and grow in a peaceful, inspiring environment.
